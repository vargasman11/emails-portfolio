What is Amazon Relay’s Short-Term Contracts program and how can my business benefit from participating?

Short-Term Contracts will allow your company to secure full work-weeks for your drivers, and grow your fleet by locking in guaranteed revenue (subject to the terms and conditions of your company’s Relay Agreement) with single or multi-week contracts several weeks in advance. Contracts offer assurance in knowing your drivers have work, without the hassle.

Carriers with a Relay Performance Score of at least 90 are eligible to participate in Relay Short-Term Contracts for the specified time period and the total contract value posted when you accept the Short-Term Contract. Access to and availability of Short-Term Contracts are based on Amazon network requirements and needs and are not guaranteed. Please refer to Amazon Relay Program Policies and Amazon Relay Carrier Terms of Service for more information.
